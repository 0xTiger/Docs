Plugin files enhance and expand the capabilities of existing software programs. They find frequent application in image, video, and audio editing tools, as well as in web browsers. These additions are often known by alternative terms such as add-ons or extensions.

Common plugin file extensions include .PLUGIN, .8BI, and .VST.
