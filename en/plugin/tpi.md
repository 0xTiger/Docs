## What is a TPI file?

A TPI file is a plugin file employed by EDIUS, a video editing application software. It enhances the video editing capabilities, typically crafted to incorporate new file formats or apply adjustments such as video color correction, lighting effects, motion blurs, and various other visual enhancements.

## About EDIU Video Editing Software

Thomson EDIUS is a non-linear video editing software developed by Grass Valley, a subsidiary of Belden Inc. It is a professional video editing application used by broadcasters, filmmakers, and video editors for post-production purposes.

Key features of Thomson EDIUS include:

1. **Real-time Editing:** One of the notable features of EDIUS is its ability to handle real-time editing of multiple video formats and frame rates. This can be particularly useful for professionals who work with various types of media.

1. **Multicam Editing:** EDIUS supports multicam editing, allowing users to edit footage from multiple cameras simultaneously. This is beneficial for projects such as live events or interviews where multiple camera angles are used.

1. **Support for Various File Formats:** EDIUS is known for its wide range of format support, including SD, HD, 2K, and 4K resolutions. It can handle a variety of video file formats and codecs.

1. **Advanced Color Correction:** The software provides tools for color correction and grading, allowing editors to enhance the visual quality of their footage.

1. **Integration with Third-Party Plugins:** EDIUS can be extended through the use of third-party plugins, offering additional features and capabilities beyond the core functionality of the software.

1. **Proxy Workflow:** It supports a proxy workflow, allowing users to edit with lower-resolution proxy files and then relink to the high-resolution files during the final export.

1. **Audio Editing:** In addition to video editing, EDIUS includes tools for audio editing, enabling users to synchronize and edit audio tracks alongside video.

1. **User Interface:** EDIUS features a user-friendly interface with customizable layouts, making it adaptable to individual preferences and workflows.
