## About DOCX to TAR

While working with Microsoft word, the document size may get huge if the contents are too long and includes items such as images and other media. DOCX to TAR is a process of compressing the word document to an archive that is small in size. This helps in sharing the large DOCX file with others on the internet as well as through online storage media such as Google Drive or Dropbox. Several online tools and apps exist that can convert DOCX to TAR. Aspose.Words for conversion is one such app that lets you convert [DOCX](/word-processing/docx/) to [TAR](/compression/tar/).
