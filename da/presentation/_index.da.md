You must be familiar with **PPTX** and **PPT** extension files, these are Presentation file formats that store collection of records to accommodate presentation data such as: slides, shapes, text, animations, video, audio and embedded objects. A presentation can be saved/converted into other file formats as well such as PDF, BMP, PNG, JPEG, and XPS.

Almindelige præsentationsfiludvidelser og deres tilknyttede filformater omfatter **[PPTX](/presentation/pptx/)**, **[PPT](/presentation/ppt/)** og **[ODP](/presentation/odp/)**.

Har du spørgsmål relateret til præsentationsfilformater? Gå videre til vores fællesskab [forums](https://forum.fileformat.com/c/presentation/8) for at drage fordel af den viden, der deles af filformateksperter.
