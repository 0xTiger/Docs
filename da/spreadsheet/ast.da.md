## Hvad er en AST fil?

En AST-fil er en regnearksskabelonfil, der er oprettet med Ability Spreadsheet-softwaren, en regnearkskomponent i Ability Office-pakken. Disse skabelonfiler bruges til at oprette et indledende sæt regnearksskabeloner, der kan bruges til at oprette [AWS](/spreadsheet/aws/) filer. AST-filer kan åbnes med Ability-regnearksoftware.

## AST filformat - flere oplysninger

AST files are saved to disc as binary files and can be opened in Ability Spreadsheet software. Though fully compatible with Microsoft XLS and XLSX file formats, AST file format specifications are not available publicly for developer's reference. AST files can be saved to disc in [PDF](/pdf/) as well as [XPS](/page-description-language/xps/) file formats.
