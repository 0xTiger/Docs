## MP4 ve MP5 arasındaki fark

To distinguish themselves from other media players, numerous Chinese portable media players market themselves as "MP5 players". In reality, there is not distinct MP5 file format; the so-called MP5 files associated with these media players are essentially MP4 files that have been renamed. Typically encoded using the H.264/AVC or MPEG-4 codec, these files can often be played on any MP4 media player by simply changing their file extension to .mp4. Esasen, bu bağlamdaki MP5 terimi, benzersiz dosya biçimini ifade etmez; bunun yerine, bu cihazlardaki MP4 kodlu içerikle uyumluluk için pazarlama etiketi görevi görür.
